= Change password in the web console

[role="_abstract"]
To ensure that your account is not accessible with the default `admin` password, you must change your account password after you log in for the first time. When you change your password for the first time, also specify your account details.

.Procedure

. Log in to the {ProductShortName} application with `admin` as your password.
. In the upper-right corner, click your role drop-down list.
. Select *User account*.
. On the *Edit* user page, enter the following information:
* Enter your name.
* Enter your email address
* Enter a new password in the *Password* field.
* Enter the same password in the *Confirm Password* field.
. Click *Save*.

.Verification

Log out of your account and log in with your new password.
